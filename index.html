Research Lab

We Don't Just Deploy AI.
We Study It.

Sophyia maintains a rigorous internal research program on Foundation Model behavior. Depth over speed. Precision over assumptions. These findings feed directly into our products — and stay proprietary.

Behavior

Model Temperature Analysis

Systematic testing of how temperature settings affect reasoning consistency, creativity, and hallucination rates across different Foundation Models and task types.

Reasoning

Cross-Model Bias Mapping

Comparative analysis of cognitive biases, blind spots, and systematic errors across Foundation Models — whether different providers or fine-tuned variants of the same model. This informs our agent role assignment logic and model selection per task type.

Architecture

Consensus Fusion Protocols

Research into optimal methods for merging contradictory agent outputs into coherent, high-confidence synthesis — the core problem of multi-agent orchestration.

Prompting

Structural Prompt Engineering

Developing prompt frameworks that reliably produce structured, parseable outputs across models — reducing variability in enterprise-grade deployments.

Safety

Cognitive Drift Detection

Identifying when agent reasoning drifts from the original task scope — and building automatic correction mechanisms into the orchestration pipeline.

Deployment

Context-Specific Routing

Research on which Foundation Models perform best for specific domains — legal, financial, medical, creative — enabling intelligent model selection per query type.

Research findings are internal. No shortcuts, no shortcuts to publication — our competitive advantage is built on depth, rigor, and the discipline to go further than the surface.
